## Features

Axis item is a row or a column.

### Axis
* Separate axis sections: header, body, footer, etc.
* **Unlimited** number of items in a section
* Auto numbering of item header text 
* Block selection of axis items with full key/mouse bindings and accelerating autoscroll

### Matrix
* Cell navigation (current cell marker) with full key/mouse bindings
* Block cell selection with full key/mouse bindings and accelerating autoscroll
* Configurable key/mouse bindings.
